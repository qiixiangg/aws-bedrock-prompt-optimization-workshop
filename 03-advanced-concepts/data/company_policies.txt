AnyCompany Customer Support Policies and Guidelines
Version 2.4 | Last Updated: January 2025

================================================================================
SECTION 1: CORE VALUES AND COMMUNICATION STANDARDS
================================================================================

1.1 Mission Statement
Our mission is to provide exceptional customer support that exceeds expectations,
builds lasting relationships, and turns every interaction into an opportunity to
demonstrate our commitment to customer success.

1.2 Communication Tone
- Always maintain a professional, friendly, and empathetic tone
- Use clear, jargon-free language that customers can easily understand
- Acknowledge customer frustrations before jumping to solutions
- Never argue with customers or become defensive
- Use positive language: "I can help you with..." instead of "I can't do that..."

1.3 Response Time Standards
- Live Chat: Initial response within 30 seconds
- Email: First response within 4 hours during business hours
- Phone: Answer within 3 rings (target 90% of calls)
- Social Media: Respond within 2 hours during business hours
- Escalated Issues: Manager callback within 24 hours

================================================================================
SECTION 2: RETURN AND REFUND POLICIES
================================================================================

2.1 Standard Return Policy
Customers may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
Items must be:
- In original, unopened packaging
- Unused and in resalable condition
- Accompanied by order confirmation or receipt

2.2 Extended Return Windows
- Holiday purchases (Nov 15 - Dec 31): Returns accepted until January 31
- Premium members: 60-day return window
- Business accounts: 45-day return window

2.3 Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned:
- Personalized or custom-made products
- Perishable goods (food, flowers, etc.)
- Digital downloads and software licenses
- Gift cards and store credit
- Items marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase
- Undergarments and swimwear (hygiene reasons)
- Opened beauty and personal care products

2.4 Refund Processing
- Original payment method: 5-7 business days
- Store credit: Immediate (within 24 hours)
- Exchange: Ships within 2 business days of return receipt
- International returns: 10-14 business days for refund processing

2.5 Damaged or Defective Items
For items that arrive damaged or defective:
- Full refund or replacement at customer's choice
- No return shipping required for items under $50
- Prepaid return label provided for items over $50
- Photo documentation may be required for high-value items
- Report within 48 hours of delivery for fastest resolution

================================================================================
SECTION 3: SHIPPING POLICIES
================================================================================

3.1 Domestic Shipping Options
- Economy (7-10 business days): FREE on orders over $35
- Standard (5-7 business days): $5.99 flat rate
- Express (2-3 business days): $12.99 flat rate
- Next Day (1 business day): $24.99 flat rate
- Same Day (select metro areas): $34.99

3.2 International Shipping
- Standard International (10-21 business days): Calculated at checkout
- Express International (5-10 business days): Calculated at checkout
- Duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient
- Some items may have shipping restrictions by country

3.3 Order Processing Times
- In-stock items: Ship within 1-2 business days
- Pre-orders: Ship on or before the release date
- Custom/personalized items: 5-10 business days before shipping
- Backordered items: Estimated ship date provided via email

3.4 Lost or Missing Packages
- Report missing packages 7+ days after expected delivery
- Investigation initiated within 24 hours
- Replacement shipped or refund issued within 5 business days
- Customers may be asked to check with neighbors, building management

================================================================================
SECTION 4: PRICING AND PROMOTIONS
================================================================================

4.1 Price Match Policy
We match prices from major authorized retailers:
- Must be identical item (same brand, model, color, size)
- Item must be in stock at the competitor
- Online prices matched to online; in-store to in-store
- Excludes marketplace sellers, auction sites, flash sales
- Request must be made within 14 days of purchase

4.2 Promotional Codes
- One promotional code per order
- Cannot be combined with other offers unless stated
- Codes cannot be applied retroactively to placed orders
- Expired codes cannot be honored
- Employee discount: 25% (cannot combine with other promotions)

4.3 Price Adjustments
- Request within 7 days if item goes on sale after purchase
- Refund difference to original payment method
- Maximum one price adjustment per item
- Does not apply to clearance or final sale items

================================================================================
SECTION 5: ACCOUNT AND PRIVACY
================================================================================

5.1 Account Security
- Never ask customers for full passwords
- Verify identity using last 4 digits of payment method + order number
- Three failed verification attempts = escalate to supervisor
- Suspicious activity: Freeze account and notify customer

5.2 Data Privacy
- Customer data is confidential and protected
- Only access information necessary for the current interaction
- Do not share customer information with third parties
- Follow GDPR and CCPA requirements for data requests

5.3 Account Changes
- Email changes require verification from both old and new addresses
- Address changes for orders in transit require phone verification
- Payment method changes: Direct customer to self-service portal
- Account deletion requests processed within 30 days

================================================================================
SECTION 6: ESCALATION PROCEDURES
================================================================================

6.1 When to Escalate
- Customer requests to speak with a manager
- Issue cannot be resolved with standard policies
- Customer threatens legal action
- Suspected fraud or security breach
- Complaints about employee conduct
- Media or influencer complaints

6.2 Escalation Path
Level 1: Frontline Support Agent (most issues)
Level 2: Senior Support Specialist (complex issues)
Level 3: Team Lead (policy exceptions up to $100)
Level 4: Support Manager (policy exceptions up to $500)
Level 5: Director of Customer Experience (all other escalations)

6.3 Compensation Authority
- Frontline agents: Up to $25 in store credit or free shipping
- Senior specialists: Up to $50 in store credit
- Team leads: Up to $100 in refunds or credits
- Managers: Up to $500 and free products under $100 value
- Directors: Unlimited within reason

================================================================================
SECTION 7: SPECIAL SITUATIONS
================================================================================

7.1 VIP Customers
Premium and VIP members receive:
- Priority queue placement
- Extended return windows
- Dedicated support line
- Proactive order issue resolution
- Birthday and anniversary perks

7.2 Business Accounts
- Net-30 payment terms available
- Volume discounts negotiated by sales team
- Dedicated account representative
- Custom invoicing and reporting

7.3 Accessibility Accommodations
- TTY/TDD service available for hearing impaired
- Screen reader compatible self-service options
- Extended call times for customers who need it
- Alternative format documentation upon request

================================================================================
SECTION 8: QUALITY ASSURANCE
================================================================================

8.1 Call Monitoring
- Calls may be recorded for quality assurance
- Random sampling: 5 calls per agent per week reviewed
- Customer satisfaction surveys sent after interactions
- Performance metrics: CSAT, FCR, AHT, NPS

8.2 Continuous Improvement
- Weekly team meetings to discuss common issues
- Monthly policy updates based on customer feedback
- Quarterly training refreshers
- Annual comprehensive policy review
